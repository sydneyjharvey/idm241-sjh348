Etsy Cart Button Microinteraction - Beta

https://www.etsy.com/

Triggers:

When the mouse hovers over or off the cart icon.

Rules:

When hovering over the cart icon, A gray circle centered behind cart goes from width:0% to width:100%, then to width:91% as a pulse animation. A pointer graphic labeled “Cart” goes from opacity:0% to opacity:100% and moves 30px down, then back up 5px as a bounce animation. The cart rotates to -15deg, then to 15deg, then to 0deg as a wobble animation. A box containing preview images of the items in the user's cart slides in from the left and grows in opacity from 0% to 100%. These animations reverse when hovering off the cart, but do not loop.
