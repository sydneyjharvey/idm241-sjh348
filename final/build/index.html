    Triggers:

    • Upon hover on, cart icon.
    • Upon hover off, cart icon or cart items.
    • Upon click, cart item add or subtract button.
    • Upon click, cart icon.

    Rules:

    • When hovering over the cart icon, a gray circle centered behind cart goes from width:0% to width:100%, then to width:91% as a pulse animation.
    • When hovering over the cart icon, a pointer graphic labeled “Cart” goes from opacity:0% to opacity:100% and moves 30px down, then back up 5px as a bounce animation.
    • When hovering over the cart icon, the cart rotates to -15deg, then to 15deg, then to 0deg as a wobble animation.
    • When hovering over the cart icon, a box containing preview images of the items in the user's cart slides in from the left, grows in width, and grows in opacity from 0% to 100%.
    • When a preview image is hovered over with the mouse, an overlay appears over it stating information on that item.

    These animations reverse when hovering off the subject, be it the preview images or the cart, but do not loop.

    • When either the add or subtract item button is clicked on, a single item of that type is added or removed from the cart. The item overlay, the item amount, item total price, and cart item amount change to reflect this.
    • When the cart icon is clicked on, it will lover in opacity by 50% before growing back to 100% opacity. This animation loops until the user is redirected to Etsy’s cart page in a new tab, then stops looping.
