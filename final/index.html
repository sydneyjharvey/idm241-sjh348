Etsy Cart Button Microinteraction - Final

https://www.etsy.com/

Triggers:

When the mouse hovers over or off the cart icon. When the mouse hovers over and off a cart item icon. When the mouse clicks on the add or remove cart item buttons. When the mouse clicks on the cart icon.

Rules:

When hovering over the cart icon, A gray circle centered behind cart goes from width:0% to width:100%, then to width:91% as a pulse animation. A pointer graphic labeled “Cart” goes from opacity:0% to opacity:100% and moves 30px down, then back up 5px as a bounce animation. The cart rotates to -15deg, then to 15deg, then to 0deg as a wobble animation. A box containing preview images of the items in the user's cart slides in from the left and grows in opacity from 0% to 100%. When hovering over a cart item image, the image will turn semi-opaque and the item name, unit number, price per unit, total price, and add and remove item buttons will fade in. When clicking on the add or the remove item buttons, the button will briefly turn white in a pulse animation, and the unit number, total price, and total unit number will instantly change accordingly. These animations reverse when hovering off the cart, but do not loop. When clicking on the cart icon, the cart image will faintly squash and stretch in a loop. In practice this would be a loading animation to arrive at the cart page.
